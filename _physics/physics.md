# Overview

Physics analysis for ePIC is co-ordinated by the [Physics Analysis Co-ordinators](analysis_coordination.md). The co-ordinators oversee the work done in the ePIC Physics Working Groups (PWGs).

---

## Physics Working Groups

Physics analysis efforts are sub-divided into 5 different physics working groups -

* [Inclusive](inclusive.md)
* [Semi-Inclusive](semi_inclusive.md)
* [Exclusive, Diffraction and Tagging](exclusive_diffraction.md)
* [Jets and Heavy Flavour](jets_hf.md)
* [Beyond Standard Model and Electroweak](bsm_ew.md)
